About Milagres College

Milagres College, Mangaluru, established in 1848, is one of the oldest and reputed institutions in the region. Managed by the Catholic Board of Education and affiliated to Mangalore University, the college offers quality higher education with a focus on academic excellence, holistic development, and values-based learning.

About EXCSELSO’26

EXCSELSO’26 is a national-level commerce and management fest that ignites talent and leadership through competitive, creative, and collaborative events. Join us on 7th March 2026 at Milagres College Auditorium, Mangalore.

General Rules & Guidelines

FREE ENTRY
  1. Each college may register a maximum of eight (8) participants.
  2. Participants must carry their college ID card and an official permission letter.
  3. Bring laptops, pen drives, and internet dongles if needed.
  4. All teams must report to the venue by 8:30 a.m.
  5. Participation in all events is mandatory for Overall Championship.
  6. Pre-assignment tasks, if any, will be communicated the night before.
  7. Food and refreshments provided.
  8. Judges’ decision is final. Misconduct leads to disqualification.
  9. Committee can modify rules or schedules; not responsible for personal belongings.
  10. Participants must confirm registration via coordinators’ contact.
